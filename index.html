Frequently Asked Questions

Seniors 65+ can apply for a TTC Senior card online or at any Shoppers Drug Mart. Bring ID with your birthdate.

Popular areas include Gerrard India Bazaar, Brampton, and Mississauga. Stores like Patel Brothers and Oceans are well-stocked.

Contact Health Care Connect at 1-800-445-1822 and request a Hindi-speaking physician. Many clinics in Brampton and Scarborough have Hindi-speaking staff.
