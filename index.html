Welcome to Your Community

We are the Society of Indian Grandparents. We are a community to connect elderly Indians in the Greater Toronto region with each other - find resources, make friends, and share in activities that were common to experience in India but are hard to find in the West.

About Us

The Society of Indian Grandparents (SIG) is dedicated to combating loneliness among Indian seniors in the West by building a culturally resonant peer community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Seniors 65+ can apply for a TTC Senior card online or at any Shoppers Drug Mart. Bring ID with your birthdate.

Popular areas include Gerrard India Bazaar, Brampton, and Mississauga. Stores like Patel Brothers and Oceans are well-stocked.

Contact Health Care Connect at 1-800-445-1822 and request a Hindi-speaking physician. Many clinics in Brampton and Scarborough have Hindi-speaking staff.
